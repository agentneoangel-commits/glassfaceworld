About

Born in upstate New York and now based in Los Angeles, I started in graphic design and branding before finding my way into motion graphics, editing, and eventually directing. My work sits at the intersection of music, technology, and internet culture — creating visual experiences designed to travel piece by piece across platforms.

In 2013, I co-founded GoldRush with Rahil Ashruff, directing viral videos for OG Maco ("U Guessed It" — Rolling Stone's Top 10 of 2014) and Lil Yachty ("1 Night" — RIAA Platinum, 131M+ views). These early works established my signature approach: sensory overload, meme-able moments, and the collision of 90s nostalgia with digital-native aesthetics.

Today, I run Faceworld Studios and continue to direct for major artists and brands while exploring the frontiers of AI-generated art — from Impermanent Digital (2021), a first-of-its-kind evolving NFT collection, to current experiments in machine learning and visual effects.

Beyond directing, I'm a music producer and songwriter with credits on tobi lou's debut album, tracks for Young Thug and Chief Keef, and placements in OVO's Top Boy and Apple TV's Loot.
